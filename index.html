Whether it's lounging on the beach, surfing the waves, or exploring the surrounding area.

Sustainability

Our property welcomes eco-conscious travellers seeking both luxury and sustainability while relaxing in South Lombok's natural surroundings.

By using traditional building materials, we added to the authenticity of the place but also helped in preserving local culture and reducing environmental impact.

The incorporation of a lush tropical garden adds another layer of beauty to our property. We integrated some Waste Water Gardens into the landscape, showcasing our commitment to maintaining a beautiful planet.
