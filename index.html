The Property

Our oasis is nestled within the natural beauty of South Lombok. The proximity to Dondon Beach, just a short 800-metre walk away, offers our guests a wonderful opportunity to immerse themselves in the stunning coastal environment. Whether it's lounging on the beach, surfing the waves, or exploring the surrounding area.

Sustainability

Our property welcomes eco-conscious travellers seeking both luxury and sustainability while relaxing in South Lombok's natural surroundings.

By using traditional building materials , we added to the authenticity of the place but also helped in preserving local culture and reducing environmental impact.

The incorporation of a luxurious tropical garden adds another layer of beauty to our property. We integrated some waste water gardens into the landscape, showcasing our commitment to eco-friendly practices.
