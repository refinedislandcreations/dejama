Sourced from Surabaya, each container has been repurposed to serve a specific function within our resort, blending functionality with sustainability.

From the moment you step into our reception area housed within one of these containers, to enjoying a meal prepared in our kitchen, or sipping a drink at the bar, you'll experience firsthand our commitment to eco-conscious living. Even our public restrooms, nuzzled within repurposed shipping containers, reflect our dedication to sustainable practices.
